Claim your right to good health.

If you’re Black in America, you know that good health—and the high quality healthcare that makes it possible—has been out of reach for way too long. We at Black Telehealth believe that you have a right to the highest level of care, in every sense of the word.

Our Vision

We Believe

  • You should have access to the world’s best doctors.
  • You deserve to be seen as a whole person
  • You can get the help you need, when and how you need it.
  • You are capable of living a life free of illness.
  • You are worthy of care that tends to your heart and soul, not just your body.

We Care

Black Telehealth is a simple yet revolutionary idea: healthcare by and for Black people. We transform Black healthcare by connecting patients with providers who understand the Black experience and care deeply about Black health. Our community of providers take the time to earn your trust and are committed to helping you get just what you need. They can help provide referrals, connect you to community partners, or help you follow your health plan.
