Our Calling

For too long, being Black in America has meant settling for second-rate care, inferior health, and a country that can’t be bothered to do anything about it. Some call it a failure of the system and a problem without a solution. Many think it’s too difficult and depressing to figure out. We at Black Telehealth believe that change is possible [...]

We Believe

You have a right to good heath.

You should have access to the world’s best doctors.

You deserve to be seen as a whole person

You can get the help you need, when and how you need it.

You are capable of living a life free of illness.

You are worthy of care that tends to your heart and soul, not just your body.

Black Telehealth is a simple yet revolutionary idea: Healthcare by Black people, for Black people.

Your Care Team

Led by a group of premier Black physicians who understand the Black experience, our network of Black care providers take the time to earn your trust and are committed to helping you get just what you need. They can help provide referrals, connect you to local resources, or help you follow your health plan. We are here for all of your medical, mental, and behavioral health needs.
